## Decisions Made
- `Array.from(matchAll())` used instead of spread operator `[...matchAll()]` — TypeScript reports `RegExpStringIterator` can only be iterated with `--downlevelIteration` or `es2015+` target, even though `lib` includes `esnext`. `Array.from()` satisfies the compiler without config changes.
- `OUTPUT_TYPE_CONFIG` exported (not just internal constant) so `adjust-dialog.tsx` in plan 07.1-02 can import badge colour configs without duplication.
- `detectOutputType`, `parseStructuredFields`, `parseUxJourneyScreens` all exported for future testability.
- `ContentSection` interface includes `raw` field (matching `splitIntoSections()` output in page.tsx).

### Auto-fixed Issues

**1. [Rule 1 - Bug] Fixed TypeScript error: spread operator on RegExpStringIterator**
- **Found during:** Task 1 (type definitions and parsers — `parseUxJourneyScreens`)
- **Issue:** `[...body.matchAll(...)]` fails TS2802: "can only be iterated through when using '--downlevelIteration' flag or with a '--target' of 'es2015' or higher" — even with `esnext` in `lib[]`, no `target` is specified in tsconfig.json
- **Fix:** Replaced spread with `Array.from(body.matchAll(...))` — semantically identical, TS-compatible
