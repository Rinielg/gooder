## Decisions Made

- `isCurrentlyStreaming` gates on both `"streaming"` AND `"submitted"` status — `"submitted"` is the pre-first-token phase where the AI has received the request but hasn't started streaming yet. Both states mean partial content.
- `AdjustDialog` placed at `z-20` (above input `z-10`) — ensures it appears above the chat input form without z-index conflict.
- `submitAdjust()` truncates `sectionBody` to first 200 characters — avoids echoing full content in every adjust prompt, keeping chat history readable (research pitfall 7).
- `lastAssistantId` computed via `useMemo` on `displayMessages` — avoids recomputing on every render from unrelated state changes.
